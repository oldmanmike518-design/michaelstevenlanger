The Story

Many Lives.
One Direction.

Born in New York. Raised on hard work and harder lessons. Spent years moving — South Carolina, Virginia, Florida, Ohio, Texas, California — wearing every hat imaginable. Kitchens, door-to-door sales, every kind of grind there is.

Then came the international chapter. Istanbul. Dubai. Cairo. Somewhere along the way, Egypt stopped being a destination and became home. I met my wife there — and that changed everything.

At 44, most people are winding down. I'm just getting started.
